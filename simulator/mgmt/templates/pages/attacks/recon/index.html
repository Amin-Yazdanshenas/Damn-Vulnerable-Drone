Reconnaissance

The process of gathering information about target systems to identify vulnerabilities and plan further attacks.

  1. Attack Scenarios
  2. Reconnaissance

Reconnaissance Scenarios

Name Description
Drone Discovery Identifying the presence and type of drones in an area using signal detection or visual spotting.
Companion Computer Discovery Finding companion computers attached to UAVs that provide additional processing capabilities.
Ground Control Station Discovery Locating ground control stations by detecting communication signals or network presence.
MAVLink Packet Sniffing Capturing MAVLink packets transmitted over the air to analyze drone communications.
MAVLink Version Fingerprinting Determining the MAVLink protocol version to exploit specific vulnerabilities.
