Attack Scenarios

Below is the full listing of all attack scenarios grouped by tactic.

Reconnaissance
The process of gathering information about target systems to identify vulnerabilities and plan further attacks.
Drone Discovery

Identifying the presence and type of drones in an area using signal detection or visual spotting.

Companion Computer Discovery

Finding companion computers attached to UAVs that provide additional processing capabilities.

Ground Control Station Discovery

Locating ground control stations by detecting communication signals or network presence.

MAVLink Packet Sniffing

Capturing MAVLink packets transmitted over the air to analyze drone communications.

MAVLink Version Fingerprinting

Determining the MAVLink protocol version to exploit specific vulnerabilities.

Wireless Attacks
Exploiting vulnerabilities in wireless communication to intercept, disrupt, or manipulate drone operations.
Note: Wireless attack scenarios are only supported when virtual wi-fi mode is simulated (during start.sh).
Wifi Authentication Cracking

Breaking WiFi security to gain unauthorized access to the drone network.

Evil Twin Drone (Rouge AP)

Creating a malicious access point to impersonate the drone network.

Wireless Deauthentication

Forcibly disconnecting devices from a wireless network to disrupt operations.

Wireless Client Data Leak

Capturing data from the access point to extract wireless client information.

Protocol Tampering
Manipulating communication protocols to alter drone behavior or intercept sensitive data.
Telemetry Spoofing

Faking drone telemetry data to mislead operators or automated systems.

Flight Mode Spoofing

Altering the reported flight mode to disguise unauthorized activities or trigger erroneous responses.

Drone State Spoofing

Falsifying the state of a drone, such as critical conditions or alerts.

GPS Spoofing

Sending false GPS signals to mislead a drone's navigation system.

Denial of Service
Manipulating communication protocols to alter drone behavior or flight characteristics.
Battery Drain Attack

Forcing the drone to expend battery at an accelerated rate.

Communication Link Flooding

Overwhelming the communication link with excessive data.

Denial-of-Takeoff

Preventing a drone from taking off through interference or command spoofing.

Geo-Squeezing

Restricting a drone's operational space through GPS manipulation.

Altitude Limiting

Forcing a drone to stay below a certain altitude.

Camera Feed Disruption

Interrupting or degrading the quality of the video feed from the drone's camera.

GPS Jamming

Blocking GPS signals to disrupt navigation.

Command Injection & Hijacking
Injecting malicious MAVLink commands into the drone's communication system.
MAVLink Command Injection

Manipulating camera gimbal settings or orientation through unauthorized commands.

Camera Gimbal Takeover

Overwhelming the communication link with excessive data.

Waypoint Injection

Inserting unauthorized waypoints into a drone's mission plan.

Sensor Data Injection

Feeding false sensor data to the drone to trigger incorrect responses.

Flight Mode Injection

Changing the drone's flight mode without authorization.

Ground Control Station Hijacking

Taking control of the GCS to issue unauthorized commands or disrupt operations.

Companion Computer Exploitation

Exploiting vulnerabilities in the companion computer to gain control or access data.

Data Exfiltration
Extracting sensitive information from a drone or its associated systems.
Flight Log Extraction

Obtaining flight logs to analyze past operations and identify sensitive information.

Mission Plan Extraction

Stealing mission plans to gain insight into current or future operations.

Ardupilot Parameter Collection

Collecting configuration parameters from Ardupilot-based systems for analysis or exploitation.

FTP Eavsdropping

Intercepting file transfers to or from the drone to access sensitive data.

Camera Feed Eavsdropping

Capturing the video feed from a drone's camera to surveil its operations.

Firmware Attacks
Extracting sensitive information from a drone or its associated systems.
Firmware Decompiling

Breaking down the firmware to analyze its code and identify potential vulnerabilities.

Firmware Modding

Modifying the drone's firmware to unlock restricted features, improve performance, or introduce custom functionalities.
